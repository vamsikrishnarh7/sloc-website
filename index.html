About us

SLOC -- SVCE LEAGUE OF CODERS

  SLOC is started on aiming that to make every student of our college implement his/her programmatic knowledge by contributing in mini projects and participating workshops.Learning only theory part of subjects like Data Structures,Java,SQL etc.. just for the purpose of end theory exams and leaving them off is not a good practice.Implementation of that particular topic is most important thing so that a student knows how to build a logic and he/she can able to crack any technical tests.Keeping this in mind we, students of III B.Tech CSE-C of SVEC took an initiative to start a coding club SLOC in collabaration with "Clueless Community" which provides free hands on training for all the students of SVCE.
  Our team is well prepared and each of us are proficient in various Programming languages,Data Structures and Web Development etc..
